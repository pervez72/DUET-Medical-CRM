Proactive Care in Action

The Challenge: A fast-spreading flu began moving through campus. Normally, the Medical Center would only realize the scale of the outbreak after wait times became unmanageable.

The AI Solution: Our new system's AI detected the sharp rise in flu-like symptoms from appointment data, long before it became a crisis. It flagged the anomaly to the medical administration automatically.

The Outcome: The DUET Medical Center was able to get ahead of the outbreak. They sent a campus-wide health alert, ordered extra flu test kits, and allocated staff to high-need areas, reducing the overall impact and keeping the community healthier.
